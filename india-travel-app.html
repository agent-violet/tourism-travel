⚠️ Common Tourist Scams
🚕 Taxi Overcharging
Meter "broken" scam. Always use Ola/Uber or pre-paid taxis from airports.
💎 Gem Export Scam
Strangers offer to help "export gems duty-free." Never engage — always a scam.
🏛️ Fake Guides
Unlicensed guides at monuments charging 10x. Hire only from official counters.
🙏 Blessing Scam
Priests tie thread on your wrist then demand large "donation."
🏨 Hotel Bait & Switch
"Your hotel closed/burnt down" — drivers take you to overpriced alternatives.
💊 Drugging Scam
Never accept food/drinks from strangers on trains or buses.
